Privacy Policy for Caption for Creators

Last Updated: 08/29/2025

Caption for Creators (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or contact details) from users.

Our app may access:

  • Microphone / Audio – only to process and generate captions from audio.
  • Photos / Videos – if you choose to upload or edit videos with captions.

This information stays on your device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

Any audio, video, or captions you create are used solely within the app to provide the captioning feature. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

  • All processing is done locally on your device, unless you choose to export or share your video.
  • We do not maintain a database of your personal content.

4. Third-Party Services

If the app integrates third-party tools (such as analytics, crash reporting, or ads), these services may collect limited data as described in their own privacy policies.

Examples may include:

  • Apple App Store services (for updates, purchases)
  • Analytics providers (if enabled in future versions)

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke microphone or photo/video access at any time through your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all content stored locally on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the “Last Updated” date above.
